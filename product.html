Frequently asked questions

The following are frequently asked questions about the products we distribute to you.

What is a persistence bootable fdisk?

Flash disk products that we release to the public, the feature is that the operating system runs live and can be read and written.The operating system is of course <<Hack|Track GNU/Linux itself.

Does the price include postage?

Price does not include postage.

What is serial product?

Serial product is a unique code in each product we distribute. Our goal is to ensure that the product is completely reliable, the authenticity of what we produce.

Can i cancel my order?

Of course, I can. But if the product has been sent, sorry you can't.
